Intimate Performances

Chamber Concert Series

Small-venue Indian classical music — up close with the artist. Max 50–70 seats per concert. Join our list and we'll notify you the moment tickets open for the next event.

The Experience

Music the Way It Was Meant to Be Heard

Our Chamber Concert Series brings you within feet of the artist — no large auditorium, no distance. Just a living room atmosphere with world-class Carnatic and Hindustani musicians.

🎵

Intimate Venue

50–70 seats maximum, depending on the artist. Every seat is close. Every note is personal.

📋

Advance Reservation

Seats are reserved — walk-ins are not possible. Sign up below to be notified when the next concert is announced.

🎟️

Free Entry

All AFA events are free to attend. We believe great music should be accessible to everyone in our community.
