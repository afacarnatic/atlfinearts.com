The Experience

Music the Way It Was Meant to Be Heard

Our Chamber Concert Series brings you within feet of the artist — no large auditorium, no distance. Just a living room atmosphere with world-class Carnatic and Hindustani musicians.

🎵

Intimate Venue

50–70 seats maximum, depending on the artist. Every seat is close. Every note is personal.
