Where Your Donation Goes

Every Contribution Makes a Difference

AFA is an informal, community-driven organization. We don't charge admission to our events because we believe art should be accessible to everyone. Your support helps us cover the real costs of bringing Indian classical arts to Atlanta.

🎵
Concert Venues & Equipment Sound systems, venue rentals, and stage setup for our events
🎓
Summer Camps Materials, space, and resources for week-long music camps for children
🎻
Artist Support Honorariums for visiting musicians and travel assistance for performers
🌱
Community Growth Outreach, marketing, and expanding our programs to reach more families
