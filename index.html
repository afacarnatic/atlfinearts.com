Who We Are

Student-founded.
Community-rooted.

Founded in 2022 by a dedicated group of young Carnatic musicians, Atlanta Fine Arts is a non-profit organization built on the belief that Indian classical arts deserve a vibrant home in Atlanta. We create platforms for artists of all levels to share their skills, learn, and grow — all driven entirely by volunteers.

Past & Upcoming

Our Events

2024

April 28, 2024

Chithirai Kalotsavam 2024

A vibrant celebration of Carnatic music through vocal and dance recitals, featuring two epic choirs — a first for our area — plus a special tribute to Tamil Carnatic composers.

2024

February 4, 2024

Thyagaraja Aradhana 2024

Annual celebration honoring Saint Thyagaraja featuring a group performance of the Pancharatnams — his five most treasured compositions — alongside special youth performances.

2023

October 8, 2023

Navaratri Kalotsavam

A spectacular showcase of teacher-led vocal and dance group performances bringing together artists from across the Atlanta community.

2023

July 24–29, 2023

Carnatic Music Summer Camp

A week-long immersive camp for children to explore Carnatic vocal music, taught by talented young artists from Atlanta, culminating in a group showcase.

2023

April 16, 2023

Chithirai Utsavam

A music event celebrating compositions by Tamil and Malayalam composers, with engaging audience trivia between performances. AFA's first large-scale event.

2023

January 21, 2023

Thyagaraja Aradhanai 2023

AFA's inaugural annual event honoring Sri Thyagaraja through the singing of Pancharatna kritis. The beginning of a cherished Atlanta tradition.
